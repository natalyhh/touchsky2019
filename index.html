Brazilian Market Facts

Solar Radiance

With a daily solar radiance: 4.500 to 6.300 Wh/m² (Atlas Brasileiro de Energia Solar), it is 5th place in world solar radiance potential.

Increasing Market

By 2040, PV rooftop in Brazil is expected to account for 20% of the country electricity (Bloomberg New Energy Outlook 2017)

Country Policies

PV has entered the country political agenda, with the Brazilian Ten Year Energy Expansion Plan issued in July 2017 projecting 13GW of solar installed capacity by 2026.

Positive Regulation

Recent regulation of the market has set a positive framework for the sector to operate in a healthy and self-sustained way.

Annual Growth

In 2018, Brazil has reached the milestone of a total installed PV power of 2 GW connectec to the grid.

Future Estimates

In 2019, it is expected accelerated growth surpassing 3 GW by the end of the year.
