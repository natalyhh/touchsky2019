About us

TOUCHSKY is UNIMAGNA METALÚRGICA LTDA. solar business division. Our goal is to develop, manufacture and commercialize accessible cleantech solutions for the Brazilian market. Our strategy is based on three pillars: technology, strategic partnerships and local development.

Vision

Expertise
